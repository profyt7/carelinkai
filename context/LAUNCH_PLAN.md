## Mission
To simplify senior care placement through AI-powered matching, transparent pricing, and direct connections between families and care providers.

## Geographic Strategy
- **Phase 1 (Month 1-6):** Cleveland Metro - 15 sign-ups, 6-10 paying
- **Phase 2 (Month 7-12):** Ohio Statewide - 100 sign-ups, 60-80 paying
- **Phase 3 (Year 2):** Midwest (5 states)
- **Phase 4 (Year 3+):** National

## Target Users (3 Personas)
1. **Operators** - Assisted living facility owners/managers ($49-$299/mo subscribers)
2. **Families** - People searching for senior care for a loved one (free users who drive operator demand)
3. **Caregivers** - Job seekers looking for caregiver positions (marketplace users)

## Pricing Tiers
| Plan | Price | Features |
|------|-------|---------|
| Basic | $49/mo | 1 listing, basic analytics |
| Professional | $99/mo | 3 listings, full analytics, leads |
| Enterprise | $299/mo | Unlimited, API access, white-label |
| Beta/Founding Member | Lock-in $79/mo forever | Early adopter offer |

# PHASE 1: PRE-LAUNCH (Weeks 1-4) — CURRENT PHASE

## Week 1: Automation Stack & Foundation
- Set up Mailchimp (3 lists: Families, Caregivers, Operators + email sequences)
- Set up Buffer (social scheduling)
- Create CRM in Google Sheets (4 persona tabs)
- Set up Tally + Make.com (lead capture -> CRM -> Mailchimp)
- Set up Google Analytics GA4 + Search Console
- Build Cleveland facility database (150 facilities)
- Research decision-maker names for top 30 facilities
- Set up LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter accounts

## Week 2: Content Foundation
- Create /cleveland landing page
- Create 4 care-type pages (/cleveland/assisted-living, /memory-care, /independent-living, /nursing-homes)
- Create 4 neighborhood pages (Shaker Heights, Beachwood, Westlake, Parma)
- Write first 4 blog posts (Cleveland neighborhood guides)
- Submit sitemap to Google Search Console

## Week 3: Association Outreach & Content Launch
- Attend OALA event (Ohio Assisted Living Association)
- Connect with WRAAA (Western Reserve Area Agency on Aging)
- Connect with Benjamin Rose Institute
- Publish blog posts 1-4
- Write blog posts 5-8

## Week 4: Facility Outreach Preparation
- Create pitch deck (10-15 slides)
- Create facility one-pager PDF
- Write outreach email templates
- Create demo video (5-10 min)
- Set up Calendly + Zoom integration
- Set up cold email automation (Mailmeteor)
- Publish blog posts 5-8, write 9-12

- Send personalized emails to 30 Cleveland facilities

- Conduct 10-15 demos (virtual or in-person)
- Email 30 more facilities (60 total)

- Beta pricing offer: "Lock in $79/month forever"

## Week 7: Hospital Outreach
- Contact Cleveland Clinic, University Hospitals, MetroHealth discharge planners
- Collect testimonials from beta users
- Email 30 more facilities (90 total)
- **Goal: 10-12 beta sign-ups cumulative**

## Week 8: Beta Conversion Push
- Convert free beta users to paid
- Email: "Beta pricing ends this week—lock in $99/month forever"

- Official pricing announcement + Founding Member offer
- **Go/No-Go Decision: Launch paid if 10+ active users, >40% positive feedback**
- **MRR Target: $720-$1,200**

# User Acquisition Strategy

## Primary Channel: Direct Outreach
- 120+ Cleveland facilities contacted by end of Week 8
- In-person visits for highest-value prospects
- Personalized email sequences via Mailmeteor
- Follow-up cadence: email -> call -> in-person

## Secondary Channels
- SEO: Cleveland/Ohio landing pages + blog content (long-term)
- Associations: OALA membership, WRAAA partnership
- Hospitals: Discharge planner referrals (Cleveland Clinic, UH, MetroHealth)
- Press: Cleveland Plain Dealer, Crain's Cleveland, local TV (WKYC, WEWS, WJW)
- Google Ads: Family-focused, $200 initial budget (Weeks 11-12)

## Beta Strategy
- Free beta access to first 15 facilities
- Convert to paid at Week 8
- Founding Member pricing: $79/mo locked in forever
- Incentive: "First month free" for early converters

---

# Red Flags & Contingencies
- **Low conversion (<40% beta-to-paid):** Review pricing, extend beta, offer deeper discounts
- **High churn (>7% monthly):** Exit interviews, proactive success calls, win-back campaign
- **Insufficient families:** Increase Google Ads, more hospital partnerships, lead magnets (PDF guides)

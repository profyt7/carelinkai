# 🚨 Red Flags & Contingencies

### Low Conversion (<40% beta-to-paid)
- Review pricing (is $99/month too high?)
- Extend beta by 2-4 weeks
- Offer deeper discounts (40-50% Year 1)

### High Churn (>7% monthly)
- Exit interviews with ALL churned customers
- Proactive customer success (monthly check-ins)
- Win-back campaign

### Insufficient Families
- Increase Google Ads budget
- Partner more with discharge planners
- Create lead magnets (PDF guides)

### Competitor Enters Market
- Week 1: Don't panic, assess threat
- Week 2-4: Differentiate & double down
- Focus on service, relationships, local expertise

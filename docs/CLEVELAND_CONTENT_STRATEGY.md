## Executive Summary

This document outlines CareLink AI's content marketing and SEO strategy for dominating the Cleveland senior living market, then expanding to Ohio and beyond. The strategy focuses on **hyper-local content**, **long-tail SEO keywords**, and **strategic partnerships** to become Cleveland's #1 resource for senior living information.

**Phase 1 (Month 1-6): Cleveland Domination**
- 24 Cleveland-specific blog posts (2/week)
- Target 50+ local keywords ("senior living Cleveland," "assisted living Shaker Heights")
- Rank #1-3 for Cleveland senior living searches
- Build 20+ local backlinks (associations, directories, media)

- Target 200+ Ohio keywords
- Rank #1-5 for major Ohio city searches
